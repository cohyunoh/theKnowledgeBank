# Amino Acids
---
**Amino acids** are *organic compounds* that contain *amino and carboxylate âˆ’$CO_2$* functional groups, along with a **side chain** specific to each amino acid.

**Amino acids** are the precursors to [[Proteins|proteins]].

When *proteins are digested or broken down*, **amino acids** are left. The human body uses **amino acids** to make proteins *to help the body*:
-   Break down food
-   Grow
-   Repair body tissue
-   Perform many other body functions
