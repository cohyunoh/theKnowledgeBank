# Nucleic Acids
---
**Nucleic acids** are *[[Polymers|polymers]]* made of [[Monomers|monomers]] called [[Nucleotides|nucleotides]]. 
There are *two types* of nucleic acids:
- [[DNA|Deoxyribonucleic Acid (DNA)]]
- [[RNA|Ribonucleic Acid (RNA)]]

These allow **living organisms** to *reproduce their complex components* from one generation to the next.
